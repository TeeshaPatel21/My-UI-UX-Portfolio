About Me

I am an aspiring UI/UX designer and student, focused on applying user-centered principles to real-world challenges. This portfolio showcases my approach to problem-solving through personal projects. My work demonstrates an ability to research user needs, analyze a problem, and design thoughtful solutions that prioritize the user.

Projects & Case Studies

Case Study: E-commerce App Design - The Perfect User Flow

Problem (UX): Most e-commerce apps have cluttered designs and confusing checkout processes. This leads to user frustration and abandoned carts. Users need a simple, intuitive, and visually pleasing way to shop online.

Proposed Solution (UI/UX): I designed a clean, minimalist UI with a logical user flow that guides users from browsing to checkout effortlessly. My solution focuses on a simple and elegant visual design, combined with a user experience that reduces friction and confusion. This project shows my ability to simplify a complex process and make it visually appealing.

Case Study: Ride-Sharing App Onboarding - A Seamless First Experience

Problem (UX): Ride-sharing apps often have a frustrating onboarding process with too many fields to fill out. This is a common point of user drop-off. A good user experience should be quick, simple, and friendly.

Proposed Solution (UI/UX): I designed a streamlined onboarding process that uses a conversational UI approach. The user is guided through the sign-up process one step at a time, with clear instructions and friendly messaging. The UI is clean and modern, using bold visuals and smooth transitions to create a welcoming first impression.
